Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
Year: c. 1503–1506
Why I like it: The mysterious smile and subtle shading make this painting endlessly fascinating.

Read more on Wikipedia.

The Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh
Year: 1889
Why I like it: The swirling sky and vivid energy capture emotion in an unforgettable way.

Jump back to the top or go to the next artwork.

The Persistence of Memory

Artist: Salvador Dalí
Year: 1931
Why I like it: The dreamlike melting clocks make you question reality and time.

This painting is a favourite for fans of surrealism.

The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch
Year: 1893 (one version)
Why I like it: The intense emotion and stark lines convey raw human anxiety.

Munch produced several versions — this is one of the most famous.

Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer
Year: c. 1665
Why I like it: The intimacy of the portrait and the luminous pearl create a quiet intensity.

The painting is sometimes called the "Mona Lisa of the North."
